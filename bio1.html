Muhammad Ali

"Muhammad Ali: The Greatest of All Time" chronicles the extraordinary life and career of the legendary boxer, activist, and cultural icon. Crafted by esteemed writers and historians, this comprehensive biography captures the essence of Muhammad Ali's unparalleled impact on sports, society, and the world at large. From his humble beginnings in Louisville, Kentucky, to his meteoric rise to fame as the heavyweight champion of the world, "The Greatest of All Time" delves into Ali's triumphs and tribulations both inside and outside the ring. Readers are transported to iconic moments in boxing history, from his historic bouts against Sonny Liston and Joe Frazier to his legendary "Rumble in the Jungle" and "Thrilla in Manila" fights.
