Incredible HULK

"Incredible Hulk: The Gamma Chronicles" delves into the tumultuous life of Bruce Banner and his monstrous alter ego, the Hulk. From his tragic origins to his relentless pursuit of a cure, "The Gamma Chronicles" explores Bruce Banner's journey through the darkest corners of his own psyche. Readers are transported to the explosive battlegrounds where the Hulk battles against the military, super villains, and even other heroes, all while grappling with the inner turmoil of his own existence. But "The Gamma Chronicles" is more than just a tale of destruction and chaos.
