"Silent Sovereign: The Saga of Black Bolt" unveils the epic tale of Marvel Comics' enigmatic monarch, Black Bolt, whose voice is as powerful as it is deadly. From his royal lineage to his ascension as the ruler of the enigmatic Inhumans, "Silent Sovereign" explores Black Bolt's journey through the intricate web of politics, power, and personal sacrifice. Readers are transported to the secretive city of Attilan and beyond as Black Bolt navigates the treacherous waters of diplomacy and warfare, all while wrestling with the inner turmoil of his own forbidden voice.
