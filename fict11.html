The Raven & the Rush

In the mystical realm of Eldoria, where magic and intrigue intertwine, a young woman named Lyra finds herself thrust into a deadly game of politics and power as she navigates the treacherous court of the Raven King. As the daughter of a noble family, Lyra has spent her life navigating the intricate web of alliances and rivalries that define the court of the Raven King. But when a series of mysterious murders rocks the kingdom, Lyra finds herself drawn into a conspiracy that threatens to unravel the very fabric of society. With the help of a brooding mercenary named Rylan, Lyra embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind the murders and bring the perpetrators to justice. Along the way, she must navigate a dangerous world of dark magic, forbidden romance, and deadly betrayals, all while struggling to uncover the secrets of her own past.
