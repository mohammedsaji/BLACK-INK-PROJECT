Iron Fist

"Iron Fist: The Living Weapon" immerses readers in the mystical and martial arts-filled world of Danny Rand, the Immortal Iron Fist. Crafted by talented comic creators, this series showcases Danny's journey as he harnesses the power of the ancient martial arts of K'un-Lun to become a living weapon against evil. In "The Living Weapon," Danny Rand grapples with the legacy of his family and the responsibilities that come with being the Iron Fist. From battling ancient foes to protecting the innocent on the streets of New York City, Danny's path is filled with danger, intrigue, and profound self-discovery. But "The Living Weapon" is more than just a tale of superheroics; it's a deep dive into the rich mythology and spiritual traditions of K'un-Lun.
