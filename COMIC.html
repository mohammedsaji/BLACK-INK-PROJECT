MARVEL COMICS

Marvel Comics, an iconic American comic book publisher, has left an indelible mark on popular culture since its founding in 1939. Its roster of beloved characters, including Spider-Man, Iron Man, and the Avengers, has become synonymous with storytelling excellence and cultural significance. Marvel's commitment to diversity and inclusion is evident in its expansive universe, where characters from various backgrounds and walks of life come together to embark on thrilling adventures. Beyond the comic book pages, Marvel's multimedia franchise, spanning films, television, and merchandise, has captivated audiences of all ages, solidifying its status as a global entertainment powerhouse. With its innovative storytelling and unwavering dedication to representing diverse voices, Marvel continues to inspire and resonate with fans worldwide, shaping the landscape of modern storytelling for generations to come.

SPIDER-MAN GANG-WAR

In AMAZING SPIDER-MAN: GANG WAR, Peter Parker calls in his fellow Spider-Man Miles Morales, She-Hulk, Daredevil, Spider-Woman, and Luke Cage to help put an end to the gang war ravaging the streets of their beloved city. This gargantuan task puts them up against the likes of Madame Masque, Hammerhead, Black Mariah, Mr. Negative, and so many more villains, all of whom would be more than happy to knock out a couple of annoying super heroes while they're eliminating their rivals. Now, in a special accelerated release, the first half of AMAZING SPIDER-MAN: GANG WAR has swung onto Marvel Unlimited! Beginning with THE AMAZING SPIDER-MAN (2022) #37, Marvel Unlimited subscribers can enjoy the first fifteen issues in this Spidey saga a few months early.

X-MEN 97

The first trailer for Marvel Animation’s X-Men ’97 has arrived, and the all-new series features 10 episodes and begins streaming on March 20 on Disney+. X-Men ’97 revisits the iconic era of the 1990s as The X-Men, a band of mutants who use their uncanny gifts to protect a world that hates and fears them, are challenged like never before, forced to face a dangerous and unexpected new future. The voice cast includes Ray Chase as Cyclops, Jennifer Hale as Jean Grey, Alison Sealy-Smith as Storm, Cal Dodd as Wolverine, JP Karliak as Morph, Lenore Zann as Rogue, George Buza as Beast, AJ LoCascio as Gambit, Holly Chou as Jubilee, Isaac Robinson-Smith as Bishop, Matthew Waterson as Magneto and Adrian Hough as Nightcrawler. I bring this up only because it reminded me a bit of Kneel Before Zod, the newly launched Joe Casey and Dan McDaid comic that features the notorious super-villain in the lead role. Dru-Zod’s had an interesting time of late within the DC Universe.

INVINCIBLE IRON-MAN

SIDE BY SIDE WITH… MAGNETO! For years, the Master of Magnetism stood as Iron Man's formidable foe, a force to reckon with in every battle. However, faced with the imminent threat of Orchis, a reluctant alliance emerges. Can Tony Stark set aside past grievances and join forces with the X-Men's former adversary? As they confront the overwhelming might of every Sentinel on Earth, the clash tests the limits of their power and the strength of their resolve. In this unlikely partnership, the fate of humanity hangs in the balance, forging bonds amidst the chaos of war.

DEADPOOL THE SAGA OF WADE WILSON

Uncover Deadpool's complicated history with Weapon X! When his healing factor fails, Wade Wilson grudgingly turns to Dr. Killebrew, the mad scientist who cured his cancer - and ruined his life! But what exactly was done to Deadpool in the Workshop? And can Wade control his impulse to slice Killebrew into little pieces? Then, Ajax wants Deadpool's stain scrubbed from the planet. What is his connection to Wade's nightmarish origin - and how is Death herself involved? Plus: The resurrected Weapon X program makes Wade an offer he can't refuse: If he joins their cutthroat crew, they'll fix his ravaged face! And when Deadpool is haunted by the ghosts of his past, can fellow Weapon Plus alumni Wolverine and Captain America help him through his most harrowing battle ever?
