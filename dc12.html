Batman the Deceased

"Darkness Descends: The Fall of Batman in the Deceased Universe" unveils the haunting narrative of Batman's struggle for survival in a world ravaged by the Anti-Life Equation. From the shadowed alleys of Gotham City to the devastated landscapes of a world in ruins, "Darkness Descends" immerses readers in the relentless battle for survival as Batman fights to protect the remnants of humanity against overwhelming odds. Readers are thrust into a nightmarish landscape where every shadow conceals a new threat, and every ally could be the next victim of the insidious virus.
