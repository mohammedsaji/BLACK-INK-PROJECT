Driven by a desire to protect his homeland from the ravages of war and disease, Rylan dedicates himself to cultivating the Fantasy Roses in secret, hiding them away in the depths of the enchanted forest where they bloom in brilliant hues of crimson and gold. But Rylan's peaceful existence is shattered when the kingdom falls under siege by dark forces seeking to harness the power of the Fantasy Roses for their own nefarious purposes. As the realm descends into chaos and despair, Rylan must embark on a perilous quest to safeguard the roses and prevent them from falling into the wrong hands.
