Hawkeye

"Hawkeye: Aim True" takes readers on an adrenaline-fueled journey through the sharpshooting world of Clint Barton, better known as Hawkeye. Crafted by acclaimed comic historians, this saga unveils the gripping narrative of a man who fights with precision and determination in a world of gods and monsters. From his humble beginnings as a circus performer to his tenure as a member of the Avengers, "Aim True" delves deep into Clint Barton's journey to become one of Earth's mightiest heroes. Readers are transported to the streets of New York City and beyond as Hawkeye battles against an array of foes, using his unparalleled marksmanship skills to protect the innocent and thwart the plans of evildoers.
