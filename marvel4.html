Wolverine

"Wolverine: The Adamantium Saga" delves into the tumultuous life of the legendary mutant known as Wolverine. Crafted by acclaimed comic historians, this saga unveils the gripping narrative of a man haunted by his past and driven by an unquenchable thirst for redemption. From his mysterious origins to his tenure as a member of the X-Men, "The Adamantium Saga" explores Wolverine's journey through the shadows of his own history. Readers are transported to the wilds of Canada and beyond as Wolverine battles against his inner demons and confronts the dark forces that seek to control him. But "The Adamantium Saga" is more than just a tale of superheroics; it's a profound exploration of identity, purpose, and the enduring legacy of one of Marvel's most iconic characters.
