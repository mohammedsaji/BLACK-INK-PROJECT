Get in the Trouble

In the sleepy town of Willowbrook, two best friends, Alex and Sam, are known for their knack for getting into trouble. From daring escapades in the local haunted house to mischievous pranks on unsuspecting neighbors, there's never a dull moment when Alex and Sam are around. But when a mysterious new neighbor moves in next door, the boys find themselves embroiled in their most exciting adventure yet. Convinced that the neighbor is hiding a treasure in his backyard, Alex and Sam hatch a plan to uncover the truth and claim the riches for themselves. Armed with nothing but their wits and a homemade treasure map, the boys set out on a series of hilarious misadventures that lead them into all sorts of trouble. From dodging angry geese to outsmarting the town bully, Alex and Sam will stop at nothing to uncover the secrets of the mysterious neighbor and find the treasure before it's too late.
