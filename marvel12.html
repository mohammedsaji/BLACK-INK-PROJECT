Astonishing ANT-MAN

"Astonishing Ant-Man: Tiny Triumphs" brings readers into the world of Scott Lang, the lovable and unconventional hero known as Ant-Man. Crafted by talented comic creators, this series showcases Scott's unique brand of heroism as he navigates both the challenges of being a superhero and the complexities of everyday life. In "Tiny Triumphs," Scott Lang grapples with the responsibilities of being a father, a friend, and a crimefighter. From battling bizarre villains to balancing his relationships with his daughter Cassie and his ex-wife, Scott's journey is filled with humor, heart, and plenty of pint-sized action.
