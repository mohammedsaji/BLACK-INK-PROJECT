Jackie Chan

Jackie Chan, born Chan Kong-sang on April 7, 1954, in Hong Kong, is a globally renowned actor, martial artist, director, producer, and stunt performer. He is best known for his incredible acrobatic fighting style, comedic timing, and innovative stunt work, which have made him a beloved figure in the world of action cinema. Chan's career in the entertainment industry spans several decades, during which he has starred in over 150 films in various genres, including martial arts, comedy, and drama. He gained international fame with his roles in movies such as "Drunken Master," "Police Story," "Rush Hour," "Shanghai Noon," and "The Legend of Drunken Master," among many others.
